About Me

I am an Combustion Turbine Technician with 15 years of experience in the power industry. I have a Bachelor's of Science in Aeronautical and Industrial Technology with an emphasis in Industrial Electronics. I have a Top Secret Security Clearance as well as Unescorted Nuclear Access.

My interests range from new concepts in creating clean energy, learning new technologies of data acquisition and control of gas turbines. I also enjoy traveling and sports.

I seek to expand my knowledge base to web design and database administration. I know these skills will enhance my current career and help me build a personal business doing web development.
